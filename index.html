Products (5)

  • Virgin queens

    Virgin queens are a popular alternative to queen cells or mated queens. Because they are caged with escort bees, they are easier to transport than queen cells. We then deliver using courier services.

  • Instrumentally-inseminated queen bees

    Widen your hive’s genetic pool by using our artificially-inseminated queens

  • Mated queens

    Mated queens are the optimal choice for the beekeeper in a hurry. They are available from October to April. These queens are laying at time of despatch.

  • Queen cells

    Queen cells contain a queen and a few escort workers. After 11 to 13 days, the bees will emerge in your new hive, upon which the queen will look for a new mate.

  • Nucleus and full box hives

    Full box hives are perfect for the beginner bee-keeper, whereas Nucleus hives are for those with a little more experience.
