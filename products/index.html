Products we offer

We can advise you on what is most suitable for your needs and supply a variety of requeening options. Whether you are in a hurry or have a bit of time up your sleeve, we can help you to keep your bees buzzing and producing to maximise your returns each year.

  • Mated queens

    Mated queens are the optimal choice for the beekeeper in a hurry. They are available from October to April. These queens are laying at time of dispatch.

  • Queen cells

    Queen cells are an economical way of re queening your hive. The cells are grafted from our breeding stock and fed to produce a strong virgin queen.

  • Virgin queens

    Virgin queens are a popular alternative to queen cells or mated queens. Because they are caged with escort bees, they are easier to transport than queen cells. We then deliver using courier services.

  • Nucleus and full box hives

    Full box hives are perfect for the beginner bee-keeper, whereas Nucleus hives are for those with a little more experience.
