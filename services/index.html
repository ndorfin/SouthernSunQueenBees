Services and training we offer

Armed with years of experience, Gary has a solid understandng of both the joys and pitfalls of beekeeping. Getting good advice can be hard to find if you are starting out and Gary has an amazing wealth of bee-related and business experience, and he can guide you towards keeping a successful hive.

We’re based on the Kapiti Coast in New Zealand’s beautiful North Island.

We can help you with…

  • Hive inspections and audits

    We can do disease inspections, hive audits, staff performance audits and training needs assessments.

  • Instrumentally inseminated queens

    For those beekeepers interested in developing their breeder queen quality or simply need some laying queens!

  • Beekeeper training

    Gary has 30 years of beekeeping and business development experience and is available to train staff in areas such as disease recognition, hive manipulation and management.

  • Consultations

    We can provide advice for business planning, initiation and development. We have a very down to earth and realistic understanding of what it takes to be successful beekeepers in the current market.
